ABOUT ME

I am a passionate 3D artist specializing in 3D sculpting and character design, bringing ideas to life with intricate details and artistic precision. With a strong foundation in ZBrush and Maya, I craft high-quality digital sculptures and game-ready models that capture realism and creativity.

Beyond 3D art, I also have expertise in graphic design and motion graphics, blending design principles with dynamic visuals. Skilled in After Effects and Photoshop, I create compelling animations and eye-catching designs for various digital platforms.

With years of experience in freelancing, I am constantly pushing my creative boundaries, always eager to take on new challenges and bring fresh ideas to every project.
